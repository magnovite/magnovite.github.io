    About Magnovite:

    Magnovite is the annual techno_cultural-design fest scheduled for 28-29 February 2020. Collectively, Magnovite has 5 Technical events, 9 Management events, 6 Architecture events, 1 Cultural event and ‘Nritta’- A Mega Dance Event. Magnovite aims to be a multi-disciplinary approach to innovation, achieved through a collaborative effort of the best minds from different disciplines. In Magnovite, we curate a fest with fusion of technology and culture to make that ambition a reality.
